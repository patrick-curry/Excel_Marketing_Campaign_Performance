# Excel_Marketing_Campaign_Performance
Building on my previous marketing work, I wanted to work with a larger dataset, dig deeper and expand on marketing knowledge.

## Data:
This dataset contains detailed performance metrics for 10,000 marketing campaigns executed across multiple channels in 2025. It provides insights into impressions, clicks, leads, conversions, costs, and revenue for campaigns run via Search, Display, Social, Email, and Influencer marketing channels.
The dataset can be located on [Kaggle](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/mirzayasirabdullah07/marketing-campaign-performance-dataset).

## Marketing Performance Data Analysis
The idea for this project was to take a dataset I have never worked with and explore the new data, asking questions and building a full analysis from scratch. The overall goal was to understand campaign performance, compare channels and identify areas where performance could be improved.
Process: 
- Convert date and currency fields
- Remove duplicates, nulls and anomaly check
- Validate key metrics like ROI
- Create KPIs, Conversion Data and Funnel metrics
- Transfer data to Date Bucket

## Overall Insight:
- The company generated $51M in revenue for 2025 while spending $25.5M, achieving a 100% ROI, which indicates highly effective marketing performance.
## Channel Insight:
- Display and search channels drive the highest revenue.
- Both channels achieved 100+% ROI indicating efficient spending.
- Social has the lowest ROI of 98%. This is slightly underperforming other channels
## Cost Insight:
- Email marketing has the lowest cost per conversion, making it the most efficient channel.
- Display has the highest cost but still delivers strong revenue.
## Time Trends:
- Timelines remain stable with slight fluctuations, showing no seasonal impacts and consistency in campaign performance
## Campaign Insights:
- The gap between top and bottom performing campaigns suggest strong optimization potential through scaling high-performing strategies and   reevaluating under-performing ones.
## Funnel Insights:
- The drop-off from Impressions to clicks is significant, while the downstream funnel shows stability.

## Recommendations:
There is potential to improving ROI on Search/Display channels by diverting budget away from Social. 

Scaling Email campaigns due to the low cost could yield returns. Testing new targeting for Social could help close the ROI gap.

 Reallocating funds from underperforming campaigns to top performers could improve upon an already high performing portfolio.
